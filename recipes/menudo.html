Menudo

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 pcs potatoes, medium sized, cut in cubes
  • 1 pc carrot, medium sized, cut in cubes
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pc onion, minced
  • 250 grams pork kasim, cut into small cubes
  • 250 grams pork liempo, cut into small cubes
  • 250 grams pork liver, cut in small cubes
  • 1 (250 g) pack tomato sauce
  • 2 pcs Knorr Pork Broth Cube
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • 1 cup cubed red and green bell pepper
  • Optional: 2 bunches Bok-choi, sliced
    •

    Steps

    1. Step 1: Let’s begin by sautéing the onion and garlic in hot oil until lightly browned.
    2. Step 2: Add the pork kasim and liempo next and fry until slightly brown in color before adding the liver. Cook for 2 minutes.
    3. Step 3: Drop in the Knorr Pork Broth Cubes and give this a nice stir to dissolve completely before adding the tomato sauce, water, sugar and black pepper. Mix this well.
    4. Step 4: Now, gently place the potatoes and carrots in the pan. Continue to cook over low heat until meat and potatoes are cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
    5. Step 5: Finally, add raisins and bell peppers and just allow to cook for 2 minutes longer. If you're adding Bok-Choi, add at this point. Here’s a dish that will bring the entire family together. Pork Menudo, just like home, is always close to your heart.
